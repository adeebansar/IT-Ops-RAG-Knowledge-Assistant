OUTAGE NOTES - PAYMENT PORTAL LATENCY

Date:
2026-05-14

Incident Summary:
Users reported slow response times in the internal payment portal.
The issue affected approximately 38 users across Finance and Customer Support.

Timeline:
09:10 - First ticket received.
09:15 - Monitoring alert confirmed high API latency.
09:20 - Incident Commander assigned.
09:25 - Application Support and Database teams joined bridge.
09:40 - Database CPU spike identified.
10:05 - Query optimization applied.
10:30 - Service returned to normal.
11:00 - Monitoring confirmed stable performance.

Root Cause:
A reporting query caused excessive database load during business hours.

Corrective Actions:
Move heavy reporting jobs to off-peak hours.
Add database CPU alerting threshold at 75%.
Create runbook for payment portal latency.
Review dashboard refresh schedules.

Business Impact:
Customer Support experienced slower case handling.
Finance users had delayed payment verification.
