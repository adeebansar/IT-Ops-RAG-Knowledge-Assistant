ASSET INVENTORY AND LIFECYCLE POLICY

Purpose:
This policy defines how IT assets are tracked, refreshed, and retired.

Asset Fields Required:
Every laptop, desktop, monitor, docking station, and mobile device must have an asset tag, serial number, assigned user, location, purchase date, warranty date, and device status.

Lifecycle Status:
- In Stock
- Assigned
- In Repair
- Lost or Missing
- Pending Return
- Retired

Refresh Rules:
Laptops older than 4 years should be reviewed for replacement.
Devices with repeated hardware incidents should be flagged for early refresh.
Devices out of warranty must be reviewed before reassignment.

Inventory Audit:
A quarterly inventory audit must compare ServiceNow records, physical inventory, and manager ownership records.
Missing assets must be escalated to the IT Asset Manager within 5 business days.

Reporting:
Monthly asset reports should show aging devices, missing devices, warranty expiration, and high-risk departments.
